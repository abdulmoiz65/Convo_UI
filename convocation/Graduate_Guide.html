Graduate’s Guide

Dear Graduate,

Congratulations on completing your degree and becoming a part of the IBA’s prestigious alumni network. The following process will help you streamline the registration process for the upcoming convocation ceremony:

Note: Students who have completed their degree requirements in Fall 2024, Spring 2025 or Summer 2025 are eligible for the Convocation 2025.

Please register with the email that you are actively using and regularly check so you receive all updates regarding the convocation promptly. Also, provide an alternate email address at the time of registration.

  1. Step 1: Check your name in the list of graduating students on the homepage of the convocation website.
    Please fill in the correct information as all data submitted will be cross-checked by IBA staff.
  2. Step 2: Click the ‘Online Registration Link’ and fill up the required information. Please ensure that you fill in the correct CNIC numbers when filling out the form; otherwise, your entry may be restricted on the day of convocation.

    Once the registration application is successfully submitted, the portal will generate an ‘E-Invitation Card’. Each E-Invitation Card has a unique QR code that will let you enter the premises only once. You will not be able to use a single QR code more than once. Therefore, please ensure that you do not share it with anyone else.

    Note: Graduates will be receiving their E-Invites after the deadline along with the verification of the data.

    Please ensure that you PRINT the E-Invitation for yourself and your guests for security clearance on the day of convocation as the code(s) will be scanned.
  3. Step 3: Before registering, please ensure that all your dues are already cleared. If the online registration link does not process your request, please contact the concerned department (depending on your query, as per the portal):
Finance Clearance
fees@iba.edu.pk
Extension: 2307, 2309 & 2336
UMS Support
Team UMS
umssupport@iba.edu.pk
Extension: 2106, 1121 & 2160
  1. Step 4: If you are unable to register due to outstanding dues/observations, please clear these dues. You will be able to complete the registration process 3–5 days after your clear the concerned dues/observation.
  2. Step 5: Once the registration is complete, you will be able to get your E-Invitation Card.
  3. Step 6: The Academic Regalia (Gown, Headgear & Hood) should be picked from Breakout – 8 of the Adamjee Academic Block from November 29th, 2025 to December 5th, 2025, between 3:00 p.m. and 7:00 p.m. after a cash payment of Rs. 3200 is made (Rent: Rs. 700 and Refundable Deposit: Rs. 2500).

    Note: Please be advised that the ribbon color designated for undergraduates is Blue, while the ribbon color for graduates is Maroon. Additionally, you may collect your deposit refund if finished/faulty if you provide a physical copy of their IBA ID card.
  4. Step 7: Arrive at the convocation venue (Abdul Razzak Tabba Academic Block) at least one hour before the appointed time (kindly refer to Minute-to-Minute Program). You should arrive at the venue dressed in complete academic regalia (Gown, hood, stole, hood and head gear) – instructions for wearing the academic regalia will be communicated during gown issuance.
  5. Attendance at the convocation regalia is compulsory for all graduates attending the convocation ceremony. Graduates who are not appropriately dressed will not be allowed to enter the premises and attend the convocation.
  6. Please strictly follow the dress code mentioned below.
