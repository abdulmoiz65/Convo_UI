Who Can Attend

Graduates who completed degree requirements by the stipulated deadline and have cleared all dues are eligible to participate.

Guest Passes

Each graduating student may receive up to 2 guest passes (subject to capacity and security protocols).

About the Convocation

Convocation celebrates academic achievement and marks the beginning of your professional journey. The ceremony includes a formal procession, conferral of degrees, and addresses by distinguished guests.

  • Mandatory rehearsal for all graduating students.
  • Academic regalia (gown, hood, cap) required for entry.
  • Security checks and ID verification at the gate.

Registration

Complete the following steps to confirm your participation. Late registrations may incur a fee and are subject to approval.

1
Profile & Eligibility

Verify your name spelling, program, and completion status. Clear any outstanding dues with Accounts.

2
Form & Fee

Fill in the convocation form, select guest passes, and pay the convocation fee (if applicable).

3
Gown & Rehearsal

Collect regalia as per schedule and attend the mandatory rehearsal one day before the event.

Go to Registration Portal Registration FAQs

Guidelines

• Bring your student ID and registration slip. • Arrive at least 60 minutes early. • Large bags and restricted items are not allowed.

• Gown, hood, and cap are compulsory. • Dress formally and comfortably. • Return gowns as per instructions to avoid penalties.

• Only registered guests with passes are allowed. • Children under 12 may not be permitted, subject to policy. • Keep aisles clear for procession.
