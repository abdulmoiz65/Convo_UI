Participant Guidelines

Please read carefully to ensure a smooth and memorable ceremony.

Graduates must wear full academic regalia. Gowns and caps can be collected from the gown desk with a valid ID.

Arrive early for security checks. Follow ushers for seat allocation. Food and large bags are not allowed in the auditorium.

Official photographers will be present. Personal photography is allowed from designated areas without blocking aisles.
